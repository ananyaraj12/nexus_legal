Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information Collection

1.1 Data Provided by You: We collect your Name and Email Address to manage your workspace and identity within the Nexus ecosystem.

1.2 Workspace Content: We store project files, shared notes, and task data that you explicitly upload or create.

1.3 Usage Data: We automatically collect device information (model, OS) and log data to optimize app performance.

2. Data Usage & Purpose

Your information is used strictly for core functionality: real-time collaboration, project synchronization, and account security. We do not sell your personal information or share workspace content with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is hosted securely using MongoDB Atlas with industry-standard TLS encryption. Authentication credentials are hashed to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may request account and data removal at any time.
