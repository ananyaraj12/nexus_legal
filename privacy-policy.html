Our Privacy Commitment

At Nexus Workspace, we respect your right to privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your professional data and ensures transparency in our academic and technical ecosystem.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal personal information to facilitate your workspace experience, including your Full Name and Email Address. For our academic features, we may process specific interaction data to synchronize your tasks and project files across devices.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used strictly to provide and improve the Nexus Workspace services. We do not sell your personal information. Interaction history is used solely for localizing your experience and optimizing collaboration with group mates.

3. Data Protection

All synchronization between your device and our servers is encrypted. Security tokens and authentication data are stored using industry-standard hashing to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights & Deletion

You maintain full control over your data. You can request a complete purge of your account information and workspace content at any time.
