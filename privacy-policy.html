Privacy Policy

Your Workspace Privacy Matters

1. Data Collection

1.1 Personal Data: We collect your Name and Email to identify your workspace across different platforms.

1.2 Academic Information: For students, we collect branch and semester data to provide tailored project templates.

1.3 Usage Logs: We log technical interactions (device OS, browser) solely to optimize workspace performance.

2. Data Security & Hosting

All workspace data is stored in **MongoDB Atlas** using AES-256 encryption. We utilize TLS 1.3 for all data transfers. Your passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using secure cryptographic salt.

3. Usage & Sharing

Nexus Workspace is a productivity ecosystem. We do not sell your personal data or read your private project files for advertising purposes. Data sharing is limited only to the collaborators you explicitly invite to your projects.

4. User Rights

You have the right to request a full export of your data or permanent deletion of your account.
