Privacy Policy

Data Privacy for Nexus Workspace Society Management

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

App Name: Nexus Workspace

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Nexus Workspace ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares personal information of society members. This app is designed exclusively for society record-keeping. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Identification: Name, Phone Number, and Flat/Unit Number.
  • Contact Information: Email address for digital society communication.
  • Society Records: Maintenance logs, visitor records, and member directories.

The information requested will be used strictly for the internal administration of your residential society within the Nexus Workspace.

3. Third-Party Services

To maintain professional security and uptime, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • MongoDB Atlas (Data Storage)

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. We utilize encrypted MongoDB Atlas clusters and TLS 1.3 for all data transfers. Within the Nexus Workspace, your sensitive data is restricted to authorized society administrators only.

5. Data Deletion

In compliance with global data protection standards and Google Play's Data Deletion requirement, users can request the permanent removal of their records.
