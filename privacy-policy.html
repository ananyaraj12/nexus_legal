Your Privacy Matters

At Nexus Workspace, we are committed to maintaining the trust of our users. This policy details how your professional and academic information is handled within our ecosystem.

📊 1. Data Collection

We collect essential information to facilitate collaboration, such as your Full Name and Email Address. For specialized projects like "Scam Bust," we process data related to technical architecture and logic flows as directed by user input.

⚙️ 2. Data Usage

Data is used primarily to synchronize workspace content across devices and to allow real-time collaboration with group mates. We use high-fidelity infrastructure to ensure your project files and notes remain accessible and secure.

🛡️ 3. Security Standards

Nexus Workspace utilizes industry-standard encryption for all data transfers. We implement security tokens and hashed authentication data to safeguard your professional workspace against unauthorized access.

🗑️ 4. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your data at any time. Complete data removal can be initiated instantly through our app settings.
