🛡️ Privacy at a Glance

Nexus Workspace is built for collaboration. We believe your data belongs to you. We do not sell your personal information, and we use industry-standard encryption to keep your workspace content private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect minimal data including your email address for account identification and any user-generated content (notes, tasks, project titles) that you create within the app for synchronization across devices.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate workspace synchronization and improve app performance. We utilize standard services like Google Play Services and Vercel for infrastructure and analytics.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request complete data deletion at any time via the settings in our app or through our dedicated deletion portal.

Visit Data Deletion Page →

4. Security

We prioritize the security of your professional information. All data is transferred via HTTPS/TLS encryption to ensure that your communication and collaboration stay within your intended workspace.
