Your data is used to synchronize workspace content across devices and facilitate collaboration with group mates. All data is transferred via encrypted protocols to ensure your information stays within your workspace.

Data Removal

You can delete your account and all associated information at any time using our dedicated portal. Once deleted, your data is permanently removed from our live databases.
