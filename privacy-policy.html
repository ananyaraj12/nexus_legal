Privacy Policy

Protecting your data in the Nexus Ecosystem

1. Information Collection

1.1 Data Provided by You: We collect your Full Name and Email Address to verify your identity and maintain your workspace settings.

1.2 Workspace Content: We store the notes, project files, and collaboration data you create within the app to ensure cross-device synchronization.

1.3 Technical Data: We automatically collect device type, operating system, and IP addresses to prevent unauthorized access and improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the **Nexus Workspace** experience. This includes real-time project collaboration, cloud storage of your academic/professional materials, and security monitoring. We do not share your private workspace content with any third-party advertisers.

3. Data Storage & Security

We utilize **MongoDB Atlas** for secure data hosting. All sensitive information is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access to our databases is strictly limited to authorized personnel via secure authentication protocols.

4. Your Rights & Data Control

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. If you wish to permanently remove your account and all associated data, please visit our dedicated deletion portal.
