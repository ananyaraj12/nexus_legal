Request Data Deletion

We believe you should have full control over your professional data. Below is the process for removing your account and associated information from Nexus Workspace.

How to Delete Your Account

1

In-App: Navigate to Settings > Profile and select the "Delete Account" option. This will trigger an automatic wipe of your personal records.

2

Manual Request: Send an email to kiitnexus.in@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Please use the email address associated with your account.

What happens next?

Once a request is initiated:

  • Your profile and login credentials are permanently deactivated.
  • All workspace content, including notes and project metadata, is flagged for deletion.
  • Data is completely purged from our active servers within 30 days.
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any critical workspace data before proceeding.
