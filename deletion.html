Permanent Account & Data Removal

⚠️ This Action is Permanent and Irreversible

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers. This cannot be undone.

1

Instant In-App Deletion

  • 1. Open the Nexus Workspace app
  • 2. Go to Profile or Settings
  • 3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account"
  • 4. Tap the button and confirm your decision
2

Email Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion via email.

📩 Send Deletion Request Email
⏱️ Processing Time: Manual requests are typically processed within 48-72 hours.

🗑️ What Data Will Be Permanently Removed?

  • ● Personal Info: Name, Phone, Email
  • ● Academic Profile: Branch, Domain
  • ● Authentication: Hashed Passwords, Sessions
  • ● Saved Content: Daily Updates, Projects
