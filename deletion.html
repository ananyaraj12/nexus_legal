Account Deletion

Control your personal data in the Nexus Ecosystem

You can delete your account and all associated information at any time using one of the methods below:

1

✅ Instant In-App Deletion (Recommended)

📑 Steps to Delete:

  • 1. Open the Nexus Workspace app
  • 2. Go to Profile or Settings
  • 3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account"
  • 4. Confirm your decision to purge all data
⚡

Immediate Effect

Removed from live database instantly

🔒

Complete Removal

All files and projects permanently deleted

✔️

No Recovery

Account cannot be restored after deletion

2

📧 Email Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you must send the request from your registered email address for identity verification.

📩 Send Deletion Request Email

⏱️ Processing Time: 48-72 Hours

🗑️ What Data Will Be Permanently Removed?

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Email, and Phone Number.
  • Academic Profile: Domain, Engineering Branch.
  • Nexus Data: Workspace projects, collaborators, and shared files.
  • Auth Data: Hashed passwords, active tokens, and login sessions.
  • History: Daily Updates, interaction data, and Project Information.

Note on Anonymized Data: Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics may be retained for security and infrastructure improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you personally.
