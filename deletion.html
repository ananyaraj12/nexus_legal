Account & Data Deletion

At Nexus Workspace, we believe you should have full control over your data.

How to delete your data:

  1. In-App: Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Account.
  2. Email Request: Send an email to kiitnexus.in@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

What gets deleted?

Upon request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information and email address.
  • All workspace content created by you.
  • Application logs associated with your account.

Data is typically purged from our active databases within 30 days of the request.
