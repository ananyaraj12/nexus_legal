Account Deletion

Manage your digital footprint in the Nexus Ecosystem

We value your privacy. You can purge your account and all associated data through the following channels:

Method 01

In-App Instant Purge

1

Navigate to Profile

2

Select Account Settings

3

Tap Delete Account

"In-app deletion is processed in real-time. Once confirmed, your session will be invalidated and data will be unrecoverable."
Method 02

Email Support Request

If you cannot access the app, our support team can manually verify and remove your records.

📩 Initiate Request via Email

Typical response time: 48 Hours

Permanent Removal Scope

• Personal Identity Records
• All Linked Professional URLs
• Cloudinary Image Assets
• OAuth Access Tokens
• Workspace Collaboration History
• Hashed Credentials
