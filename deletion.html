Request Data Deletion

We respect your right to data portability and deletion. Follow the steps below to permanently remove your account from Nexus Workspace.

1

Navigate to Settings > Account within the Nexus Workspace app and select "Delete Account".

2

Alternatively, email kiitnexus.in@gmail.com with your registered email and the subject "DELETE ACCOUNT".

Note: Deletion is permanent. All notes, projects, and collaboration history will be purged from our servers within 30 days.
