Permanent Account & Workspace Data Removal

This Action is Permanent and Irreversible

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers. This cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before taking action.

At Nexus Workspace, we respect your right to control your personal and professional data. You can initiate account and associated data deletion at any time using one of the methods below:

1
✅

Instant In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest method. It triggers an automated, secure wipe directly from the app.

🗂️ Steps to Delete:

  1. Open the Nexus Workspace app.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Find the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm your choice via the popup.
  5. Your account will be instantly deleted.
⚡

Immediate Effect

Your data is removed from our databases instantly.

🔒

Complete Removal

All personal info and workspace content is purged.

🚫

No Recovery

Once deleted, your workspace cannot be restored.

Email Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request manual deletion via email.

📝 How to Request:

  1. Click the button below to open your email client.
  2. Ensure you are sending from your registered email.
  3. Include your full name and workspace ID (if known).

What Data Will Be Permanently Removed?

We purge all data associated with your professional workspace, including:

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Email Address.
  • Workspace Content: Notes, shared project files, tasks, and interaction history.
  • Authentication Data: Login sessions, security tokens (hashed).
  • Usage History: App preferences and synchronized configuration data.
